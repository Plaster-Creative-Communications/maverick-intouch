Are Pre-sales & Installation Services designed to help resellers win more business and drive innovation?

Jun 21 2022

We caught up with Head of Solutions & Services, Maverick UK, Jim Fitton about how Pre-Sales & Installation Services are designed to help resellers win more business and drive innovation into their clients’ meeting spaces.

Effective meeting rooms are essential spaces for businesses to communicate, collaborate and make decisions.

However, even today many meeting rooms are ill-equipped to deal with a modern workforce requiring support for hybrid working and effective collaboration, so creating a meeting space that’s beneficial to productive working for both in-person and remote attendees is a key priority for most businesses.

With more organisations embracing trends such as collaboration, mobile working and BYOD, it is becoming more important than ever before to ensure that meeting room technology enables a seamless experience for all participants while maintaining productivity and efficiency.

Maverick AV solutions installation and deployment services can help. Our unique combination of hardware solutions, value-added services and support enables our partners to win more business, enhance profitability and drive innovation into their clients’ meeting spaces.

Service Overview

A full suite of services, covering Smart Signage, Smart Meeting Rooms, LED & Projection technologies. Our portfolio of technologies are suitable for both Corporate and Public Sector clients, and our specialist designers hold certifications for our key vendors alongside years of experience and industry qualifications.

Using our nationwide network of technicians, we can help respond to your customer’s project requirements regardless of technical complexity, location, platform or hardware manufacturer.

We can provide a scalable, rapidly deployable workforce at multiple locations with a single point of contact for project management. We’ll assemble a qualified team of experts to fit your project requirements and timeline to ensure the highest level of execution and support services.

Installation services include comprehensive site surveys with a written report, Pre-Sales consultative expertise, specialised services for Microsoft Surface Hub including pre install network config, deployment installation and adoption services, alongside cabling and installation services for all of your clients’ meeting room needs.

Technicians available across the UK and Ireland can support your business with the deployment of Smart Meeting technologies, Smart Signage and large display technologies including Projection and LED.
