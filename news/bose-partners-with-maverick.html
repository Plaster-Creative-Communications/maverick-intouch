Bose Partners with Maverick AV Solutions

Jan 14 2021

Maverick AV Solutions, has announced a new distribution partnership with Bose Professional, focused initially on the Bose Videobar™ VB1, an all-in-one USB conferencing device that brings premium audio and video to meeting rooms.

This move sees the European AV distribution company which is part of Tech Data partnering with the innovator in professional audio and workplace solutions to introduce a cutting-edge meeting room device that delivers the Bose audio and video experience to meetings.

The Videobar VB1 will be globally available on 16 February 2021. Local support will be available through Maverick’s 20 sales teams providing virtual demonstrations, proof of concept kits and technical support.

Joel Chimoindes, Vice President for Europe with Maverick AV Solutions explains,

“Bose is a highly respected manufacturer and this product sees them enabling the meeting space with both their signature audio and a great video experience. We are seeing incredible growth in demand for our collaboration portfolio as businesses start to commit to technology for the return to workplaces. This is a great looking product and its consumer styling and plug and play set up will undoubtedly make it very popular. We’re pleased to be able to help Bose scale to meet demand in the channel and to work with our resellers to introduce it to the European market.”

“There is an increasing need for collaboration technology, especially as companies work through how they will support employees returning to work. A hybrid work environment supports this evolving workplace, whether collaborating with colleagues or meeting with customers, regardless of where they may be,” shared Hans Vereecken, Sales Manager, EMEA for Bose Professional. “Our commitment to our customers and passion for creating innovative solutions is echoed in our search for a distributor partner. We’re proud to have Maverick AV Solutions as our European partner and know their expertise in the market will provide the level of support and service customers have come to expect with Bose.”

You can book a virtual demonstration of the product with one of the European team by emailing info@tdmaverick.eu.

The Videobar VB1 is a great addition to the Maverick smart meeting portfolio, which is rapidly accelerating to help businesses enable hybrid meeting environments. The Videobar VB1’s elegant, low-profile design features six beam-steering microphones that automatically focus on voices in the room and reject noise, eliminating distractions during meetings. This all in one device is also Microsoft Teams accredited for an incredible video meeting experience.

Connectivity is simple through the single USB cable or wireless Bluetooth® device and the 4K ultra-HD camera with auto framing allows everyone to see and understand the presenter and feel more like they’re together in the same room.
