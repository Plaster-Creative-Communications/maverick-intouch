Smart Meetings

A new way to think about meetings.

Join, Bring and Select is a new way to look at meeting rooms.

Each considers how the user will engage with their platform. Is their business locked down to a specific VC application? Are they collaborating with lots of businesses on their selected collaboration network? Do they want an agnostic space to bring their own device?

Discover how you can use Join Bring Select and the best technology to enable rooms for users.

Join

The meeting space is locked to the desired platform for one touch setup and deployment.

If you want your meeting room to be seamlessly connected to your preferred VC or collaboration platform then Join is the approach for you. Invite your room to the Teams, Zoom or Meet call and the call will be displayed on your in room device at the allocated time.

Bring

The meeting space has installed audio visual technology and the user brings the platform.

This bring your own device solution set includes Barco Clickshare and the Windows Collaboration Display ranges. The user brings their laptop into the meeting spaces and starts the meeting through a simple USB-C or USB connection.

Select

The meeting space has installed audio visual technology with multiple platforms.

Select is all about creating a meeting space that has installed audio visual technology preconfigured with multiple platforms. This enables a seamless transition for end users of Google, Zoom or Teams without connecting devices.

The perfect fit for every platform

With the explosion of video conferencing and remote teams, meeting rooms and offices need to enable collaboration as well as presentation. Whether the organisation is using Zoom, Teams, Google Meet or any other VC platform, you can tailor technology with effective audio visual solutions to bring it to life in workspaces.
